UAB “Travel Union” Prohibited Activities List

Download as a PDF here

The list of prohibited activities provided is not exhaustive. UAB Travel Union reserves a right, in its sole discretion, to add or remove prohibited activities to/from this list. Each customer/business will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis and only then decision will be made. Final decision of treating customer’s activity as prohibited rests with UAB Travel Union.

THE LIST:

  • Human/animal trafficking
  • Weapons, nuclear power, drugs
  • Pornography, obscene materials, live chats
  • Gambling without operating country license
  • Unlicensed financial institution
  • Unlicensed lotteries
  • Anonymous e-wallet exchanges
  • Shelf companies
  • Illegal copyrighted content distribution
  • Get-rich-quick schemes
  • Explosives production and explosive substances production, import/export
  • Political organizations
  • Affiliate marketing (referring other users to join and building a financial pyramid)
  • Retail of counterfeit products/items
  • Multi-level marketing and pyramid schemes
  • Horoscopes, astrology services, fortune tellers, religion organisations
  • Aerospace, rocket, satellite or optical parts to army equipment related businesses
  • Non-profit organizations (NPOs)
  • Charity organizations
  • Antiques trading
